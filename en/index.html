Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about DataPick AI.

What websites does DataPick AI work on?
DataPick AI works on virtually any public webpage — e-commerce sites, news articles, job boards, social media profiles, directories, real estate listings, and more. As long as the data is visible on the page, the AI can extract and structure it for you.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Page content is only sent to our servers when you explicitly click the "Extract" button. We don't track your browsing history, collect cookies, or run any background processes. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
Do I need coding skills?
Not at all. DataPick AI is designed for everyone. Simply describe the data you want in plain English (or Chinese), and the AI handles the rest. No CSS selectors, XPath expressions, or programming knowledge required.
How does the AI extraction work?
When you click Extract, the extension reads the visible text content of the current page and sends it along with your description to our AI backend. We use Anthropic's Claude AI to intelligently parse and structure the data according to your requirements. Results are returned as a clean table you can export.
Can I export data to Google Sheets?
You can export your extracted data as CSV, which can be directly imported into Google Sheets, Excel, or any spreadsheet application. JSON export is also available for developers and data pipelines. Direct Google Sheets integration is coming soon.
