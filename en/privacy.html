Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Introduction

DataPick AI ("we," "our," or "us") is a Chrome browser extension that helps users extract structured data from webpages using artificial intelligence. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension and related services.

By installing and using the DataPick AI extension, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the extension and discontinue use.

How the Extension Works

When you initiate a data extraction, DataPick AI reads the text content of the current webpage and sends it to our backend processing service (hosted on Supabase Edge Functions), which then forwards the content to Anthropic's Claude AI for intelligent data extraction and structuring. The structured results are returned to you within the extension.

Important: Page content is only sent when you explicitly click the "Extract" button. The extension does not automatically scan, monitor, or transmit any browsing data in the background.

Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

📄 Page Text Content

When you click "Extract," the visible text content of the active webpage is sent to our servers for AI processing. This data is used solely to generate extraction results and is not stored permanently on our servers after processing is complete.

📧 User Email (Authentication)

We collect your email address when you sign up or log in. This is used for account authentication, managing your subscription, and sending critical service-related communications only.

📊 Extraction History

For Pro and Team plan users, we store your extraction results (the structured output data) so you can access them later. You can view and delete your extraction history at any time from within the extension.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be crystal clear about what we don't do:

  • ✗ We do not collect or monitor your browsing history or activity beyond pages you explicitly choose to extract data from.
  • ✗ We do not track which websites you visit.
  • ✗ We do not collect passwords, form inputs, cookies, or personal identifiers from webpages.
  • ✗ We do not inject ads, trackers, or third-party analytics scripts.
  • ✗ We do not run in the background or collect data when you are not actively using the extension.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Your data is only shared with the following service providers, strictly for operating the service:

  • • Supabase — for user authentication, data storage, and serverless backend functions.
  • • Anthropic (Claude AI) — for AI-powered data extraction processing. Page content is sent to Claude's API for analysis and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All data transmitted between the extension and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Authentication is handled via Supabase's secure auth infrastructure. While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable precautions to safeguard your information.

Data Retention

  • • Page content sent for extraction is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers.
  • • Extraction results are retained for active subscribers and can be deleted by the user at any time.
  • • Account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request account deletion by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • ✓ Access the personal data we hold about you
  • ✓ Request deletion of your data and account
  • ✓ Export your extraction history
  • ✓ Opt out of non-essential communications
  • ✓ Uninstall the extension at any time to stop all data collection

Children's Privacy

DataPick AI is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users through the extension or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was most recently revised.
