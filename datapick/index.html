Frequently Asked Questions

How is DataPick AI different from Instant Data Scraper? +
Instant Data Scraper uses pattern matching to find HTML tables — it only works on well-structured pages. DataPick AI uses Large Language Models to understand the page content semantically. It works on any page, including articles, profiles, and unstructured content. Plus, you can use natural language to specify exactly what data you want.
Is my browsing data safe? +
Absolutely. DataPick AI only processes a page when you explicitly click the extract button. We never track your browsing history, and we don't store the raw page content — only the structured extraction results. You can delete your data anytime. We're fully GDPR compliant.
What websites does it work on? +
DataPick AI works on any website — Amazon, LinkedIn, news sites, real estate listings, job boards, e-commerce platforms, and more. If you can see the data in your browser, DataPick can extract it.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes! You can cancel anytime from your account settings. No questions asked, no hidden fees. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your billing period.
What AI model do you use? +
We use a combination of GPT-4o-mini and DeepSeek V3 optimized for data extraction tasks. Pro and Team users get access to more powerful models for higher accuracy on complex pages.
